About

Basic

Basic level covers basic information that could be used to review for a test or quiz. Basic is recommended to start before moving on to advanced if you're unsure about your skills in this lesson. Basic level also reviews and explains the in-class notes as well as the homeworks, which are not covered in the advanced section simply because they are here. Basic is a good way to understand what was taught if you were absent.

Advanced

Advanced level is much shorter to basic level, however more detail is given and problems are often more challenging and require a higher level of thinking. Advanced level is recommended for studying for the AP test, or wanting a deeper understanding into the problems that are being given. Advanced level provides more detail to create a stronger understanding of the problems.
