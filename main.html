Chapter 1 - One-Dimensional Motion

Explore problems related to 1-dimensional motion involving, time, position, velocity, and acceleration. Interpret motion diagrams, position-time graphs, and velocity-time graphs. Understand the relationship between position, velocity, and acceleration, and how one can be determined from another.

Chapter 2 - Two-Dimensional Kinematics

Solve problems utilizing the 4 kinematics equations involving variables for displacement, initial velocity, final velocity, time, and acceleration. Learn to identify which type of motion is being described and which kinematic equation should be used to solve each problem.
