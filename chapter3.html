Lesson 3.1 - Forces and Interactions

Understand what forces are and how they work. Be able to identify the six different types of forces and how they are different. Learn to identify what forces are acting on a given object at any time. Draw possible motion diagrams for objects with given forces acting on them. Intepret force diagrams and vectors and how they affect an objects position and motion.

Chapter 2 - Two-Dimensional Kinematics

Solve problems utilizing the 4 kinematics equations involving variables for displacement, initial velocity, final velocity, time, and acceleration. Learn to identify which type of motion is being described and which kinematic equation should be used to solve each problem. Motion types include free fall, horizontally launched projectile, and angle launched projectile.
