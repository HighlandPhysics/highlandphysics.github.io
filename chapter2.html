Lesson 2.1 - Vector Mathematics

Determine if vectors of given magnitudes can add to certain values. Learn to add two component vectors into a resultant vector, and break a given resultant vector down into its components. Interpret different vector notations, such as polar form and cardinal directions. Understand the difference between velocity and acceleration vectors.

Lesson 2.2 - Horizontally Launched Projectiles

Use the four kinematics equations to solve for different respective variables regarding horizontally launched projectiles. Understand the similarities and differences between a horizontally launched projectile and freefall motion, and how to use knowledge of freefal motion to approach horizontally launched projectile problems. Be able to determine how horizontal and vertical velocity change over time.
